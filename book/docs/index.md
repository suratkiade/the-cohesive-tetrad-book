    <p>Mutaqin, A. Z. (2025). <em>The Cohesive Tetrad: Four Languages of Truth</em>. DOI: <a href="https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/D5S7V">10.17605/OSF.IO/D5S7V</a></p>

    <p>Ade Zaenal Mutaqin. 2025. <em>The Cohesive Tetrad: Four Languages of Truth</em>. DOI: <a href="https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/D5S7V">10.17605/OSF.IO/D5S7V</a></p>

# The Cohesive Tetrad

## Professional reading edition portal

**Quick actions**

- [Read Online (HTML)](reading-edition/)
